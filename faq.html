Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the SaathiCare giving network.

How do I donate items?
To donate, sign up as a donor, click "List an Item" on your dashboard, and fill out the details about what you'd like to give. Verified NGOs in Mumbai will then be able to browse your listing and send a request if it meets their needs.

What items are accepted?
We accept gently handled clothes, books, electronics, toys, and household utilities. We focus on items that are clean and in working condition to ensure they truly benefit the recipient.

How does verification work for NGOs?
Safety is our priority. NGOs must provide their Darpan ID and registration documents. Each application is manually reviewed by our admin team before they are allowed to request items from our donors.

Is there any fee?
CareBloom is a 100% free community platform. We do not charge donors or NGOs. Our goal is strictly to facilitate direct giving and reduce waste.
