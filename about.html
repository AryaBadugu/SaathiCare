Our Story

Bridging the Gap Between
Need and Generosity

SaathiCare was born from a simple observation: there are millions of useful items lying idle in our homes, and millions of people who could desperately use them.

Our Mission

To create a seamless and transparent platform where generosity thrives. We believe every item, no matter how small, can bring immense joy and relief to someone's life. We aim to empower individuals and NGOs to make a tangible difference.

Our Vision

We envision a world where giving is an integral part of daily life, and no useful item goes to waste. Through technology and community, SaathiCare strives to build a sustainable cycle of kindness that supports the underprivileged.

🌱SaathiCare

Connecting generosity with need through a transparent, seamless platform. Making giving a part of daily life.
